Edit Time Sheet

FUNCTION

  • The Edit Time Sheet page helps an employee to edit the Check In and Check Out timings of his / her subordinates.

ACCESSIBLE BY

  • User who have administrator access or supervisor access or manager access.

STEPS TO EDIT TIMINGS

  • From the Home page, My ESS  Edit Time Sheet
  • This will take us to the Edit Time Sheet page.

  • To enter the name of the Employee who’s swipe timing has to be changed
    • Press F2 or click on the icon to open the search window
    • Select the City or enter Name or Role or Email to search employees.
    • Click on the Filter button to display employees meeting the above search criteria.
    • To select an employee click on the Select button or click on the Clear button to clear the search criteria and results.

  • Once the employee is selected, click on the view button to view the swipe details of the employee for the current month. User can navigate to any months by using Previous & Next links
  • Click on the Check In or Check Out link on the corresponding date, which has to be modified.
  • In the pop-up window change user can modify, the Time Zone, Date, Shift and Time
  • Then enter the reason for modifying the swipe details
  • Click Ok to save the changes or click Cancel to exit without saving the changes.

Steps to delete entered swipe timings

  • Select the particular date (as done in edit time sheet steps) for which we need to delete the swipe timings

  • From the CheckIn / CheckOut pop-up screen, click on the Delete CheckIn/Out details check box
  • This will take us to the deletion pop-up screen, enter the reason and click on the Delete button to delete the swipe details or click on the Cancel button to exit without deleting swipe details
