Activity Review Management

FUNCTION

  • The Activity Approval page helps employees to verify, modify, approve or reject activities entered by their subordinates.

ACCESSIBLE BY

  • User who have administrator, Supervisor or Manager access.

STEPS TO VIEW, APPROVE AND REJECT ACTIVITIES

  • From the Home page, My ESS  Activity Approval
  • This will take us to the Activity Review Management page.
  • Click on the reporting structure to expand the employees below a subordinate. An employee can view his subordinates, but not his superiors.
  • Then click on the employee name whose activity has to be approved. This will display the day wise activity list on the right side.
  • Click on the activity that has to be approved. This will display the activity details at the bottom of the page.
  • Enter the comments if needed in the comments field. Comment is optional when Approving an activity, but it is mandatory when rejecting an activity.
  • Click on the Approve button to approve the activities or click on the Reject button to reject the activities. Supervisor/Manager can reject the activities for whole day.
  • Rejected activities will go back to the employee who entered it. That employee can modify the activities based on the superior’s comments and send it back for approval.

Note: Rejecting activity can be done for a particular day; that is when a user rejects, all activities on that day will get rejected. Individual activity in a particular day cannot be rejected.

STEPS TO EDIT ACTIVITIES

  • If a Supervisor / Manager wish to modify any activity before approving the activities of his / her subordinates. The Supervisor / Manager can edit the activities entered by his / her subordinates
  • Click on the Edit button against the activity Details. This will take us to the Edit Activity page to edit the details.
  • Make any changes in the desired fields
  • Give the reason for modification in the comments field.
  • Click on the Update button to save changes and exit or click on the Close button to exit without changes.

STEPS TO APPROVE/REJECT MULTIPLE USERS' ACTIVITIES

  • Select multiple users for multiple dates to approve all activities for the selected dates
  • Any comments entered will be applicable for all users' activities that are selected
  • To edit a user's activity click on the User Name
Note
  • The multiple user activity approval access is given to specified users
  • User does not have option to edit activities when user's are selected for activity approval
