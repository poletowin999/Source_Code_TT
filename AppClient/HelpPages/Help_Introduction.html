Tick-Tock is a web based application, which is used to track the daily activities of all agents and track their total work timings for a particular day. It also provides reports for billing or invoicing.

Tick-Tock Functions

  • Keep track of all activities and the time taken for that activity.
  • Keep track of all employees’ check in and check out time, which helps payroll to calculate attendance.
  • Supervisor / Manager can verify the activities entered by their subordinates and then approve or reject the activities.
  • Supervisor / Manager also have option to modify activities entered by their subordinates before approving.
  • It is flexible and it can be configured as per the team or project requirements.
  • Application has various reports to track the activities and generate invoices.
